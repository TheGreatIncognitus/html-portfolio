TD's 2024 Movie Ranking

The year in movies (so far!)

Dune - Part Two

A sci-fi masterpiece on every level, and a gripping payoff of the foundation paved by Part One.

Godzilla x Kong: The New Empire

A thoroughly enjoyable blockbuster that fully embraces its own absurdity. I yearn for a return to the franchise's original, darker tone, but I'll still be here for a good time such as this.

Argylle

Good fun even as the convoluted story veers into nonsense. Vaughn's style works better with a harder rating.

Ghostbusters: Frozen Empire

A decent effort to expand the lore of the franchise, but the overstuffed cast and meandering plot culminate in an underwhelming payoff.
